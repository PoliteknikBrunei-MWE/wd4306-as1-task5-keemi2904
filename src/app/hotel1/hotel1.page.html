THE EMPIRE HOTEL

Set 5 km from Jerudong Park, this lavish beachfront hotel is 21 km from Sultan Hassanal Bolkiah Stadium.


The plush rooms come with free Wi-Fi , minibars, and tea and coffeemakers, as well as prayer mats. Club rooms have high-end linens, and access to a lounge with complimentary breakfast, afternoon tea and cocktails. Some suites add whirlpool tubs and saunas. Elegant 2- and 3-bedroom villas have beach access and share a pool. Room service is available 24/7.


There's a Jack Nicklaus-designed golf course with sea views. Additional amenities include indoor and outdoor pools, beach access, and a spa, plus 6 restaurants, 3 bars and a gym.
